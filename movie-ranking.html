The Best Movies According to Karthik

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Harry Potter

I think the Harry Potter movies do a fantastic job bringing the magical world to life,
but I feel some of the details from the books get lost in the adaptation process for the sake of pacing.

Money Heist

I love how Money Heist keeps me constantly on edge with its clever twists,
although I sometimes feel the drama overshadows the heist planning.

Bison

I appreciate how Bison delivers its action and emotion with intensity,
though I feel the pacing could have been tighter to keep me fully engaged throughout.
